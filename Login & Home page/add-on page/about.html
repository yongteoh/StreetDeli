About StreetDeli

What is StreetDeli?

StreetDeli is a digital platform designed to help users discover authentic street food experiences in Malaysia. Whether you're a local or tourist, StreetDeli makes it easy to find delicious spots, plan food trips, and connect with local vendors.

About Vendor

StreetDeli empowers street food vendors by giving them visibility online. Vendors can list their stalls, upload photos, update opening times, and connect with new customers — all through a mobile-friendly interface.

About User

Users can explore food spots by location, food type, or reviews. StreetDeli helps them navigate the local street food scene with ease and confidence.
